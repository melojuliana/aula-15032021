Conhecendo o EXO

Korean pop group

Exo is a South Korean-Chinese boy band based in Seoul, consisting of nine members: Xiumin, Suho, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.o., Kai and Sehun. The band was formed by Sm Entertainment in 2011 and debuted in 2012. Theis music released and perfomed in Korean, Mandarin and japanese, primarilly incorporates pop, hip hop and R&B. The Band debuted with 12 members separated in two sub-groups: Exo-K and Exo-M. Kris, Luhan and Tao deparedt the grpup individually amid legal battles in 2014.

Exo broke its first records years after its debut and, even today after almost 10 years of career, it has been breaking records both nationally and internationally, as being the first group to have their music played in the "Dubai Fountain" which is the biggest source of dancing waters in the world, as the first group to do the closing ceremony of the 2018 Olympics in their home country. Currently the group has some sub-units, which are: EXO-CBX and EXO-SC and all its members have their solo work as singers, actors and consolidated models. The group has been on hiatus since 2019, as some of its members are doing their country's mandatory military service.
